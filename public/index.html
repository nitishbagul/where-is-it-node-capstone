About

The second car key, the membership card of you favorite gift store and many such things which are important to you but you don't need them in your day to day life and so you decide to keep them somewhere safe. However, you can't find the location of 'somewhere' at the time of action and you say to yourself -WHERE IS IT? Sounds like you?

No problem, you are at the right place.

My Areas : Create an Area where you keep your hidden treasures :) . Example: Living Room.

My Places : Now, I know its in the living room but where exactly? Create a place. Example: Wardrobe/Drawer one

My Items : As it should be. Example: Second car key, Membership cards

My Categories : Wanna go traveling and scratching your head preparing a checklist? Create a Category. Example: Travel, Personal Care, Hardware Tools
